1. Introduction

Welcome to To The Point (accessible from https://pranavblog.online/). We are dedicated to providing high-quality insights on Environment, Energy, and Agriculture. Because we value your privacy, this Privacy Policy outlines what information we collect when you visit our site, how we use it, and how we keep it safe.

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

2. Information We Collect

We believe in minimal data collection. We only collect data that helps us improve your reading experience and keep our website running smoothly. This includes:

  • Log Files & Analytics: Like almost every website on the internet, we use log files. This includes your IP address, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, and the pages you visit. This data is entirely anonymous and is used to analyze trends and see which articles our readers love the most.
  • Direct Information: If you reach out to us directly (via email or a contact form), we may receive your name, email address, and the contents of the message you send us.

3. Cookies and Web Beacons

Yes, we use "cookies." But don't worry, they aren't the kind that track your personal identity. Cookies are small text files stored on your browser. We use them to:

  • Remember your preferences and optimize your experience on our website.
  • Help our pages load faster when you revisit them.
  • Help our third-party advertising partners show you relevant content.

You can choose to disable cookies through your individual browser options. However, doing so may affect how some features of our website function.

4. Third-Party Advertising (Monetization)

To keep bringing you free, high-quality content regarding agriculture and the environment, we use third-party advertising companies (such as Monetag, Google AdSense, or similar platforms) to serve ads when you visit our site.

What you need to know about these ads:

  • These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
  • Third-party ad networks use technologies like cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons in their respective advertisements and links that appear on To The Point. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs.
  • We do not have access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

If you'd like to learn more about this practice and know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, you can visit the Network Advertising Initiative (NAI) website.

5. Links to Other Websites

Our articles often contain links to external sources, research papers, or related websites. Please be aware that once you click on these links and leave our site, we do not have control over that other website. Therefore, we cannot be responsible for the protection and privacy of any information you provide while visiting such sites.

6. Data Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. We use trusted hosting platforms and standard web security protocols (like HTTPS/SSL encryption) to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure of any minimal data we might collect.

7. Children's Information

Another part of our priority is adding protection for children while using the internet. We encourage parents to observe and guide their online activity.

To The Point does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you think that your child provided this kind of information on our website, we strongly encourage you to contact us immediately, and we will do our best efforts to promptly remove such information from our records.

The digital world changes fast, and so might this policy. We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, we advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
